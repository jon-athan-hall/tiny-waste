Now that we've been reducing our kitchen waste for a few weeks, it's time to talk about food

How much food waste do we produce?

The EPA estimates that in 2018, 63.1 million tons of food waste was generated, contributing 21.6% of the total MSW (Municipal Solid Waste). The average American household generates 338 lbs of food waste every year, making up 17% of total household waste.
