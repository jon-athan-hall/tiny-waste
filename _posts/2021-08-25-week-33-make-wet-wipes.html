What You'll Need:

  • Old cotton t-shirts or leggings. This project is perfect for stained or ripped clothing that are no longer wearable.
  • Scissors
  • Glass jar with lid
  • Cleaning solution - see recipe links below

Cleaning Solution Recipes

  • Bathroom Cleaner
  • Kitchen Cleaner
  • Carpet Cleaner
  • Glass Cleaner
  • Hardwood Cleaner
  1. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/1-gather-shirts.jpg" alt="Two tshirts and a pair of leggings" %}

    Start by gathering up some old cotton t-shirts, leggings, or any other cotton articles with the jersey knit consistency.

  2. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/2-cut-seams.jpg" alt="leg of leggins with seam cut off" %}

    Turn everything inside out and cut away all of the seams, cuffs, and collars.

  3. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/3-cut-rectangles.jpg" alt="t-shirts and leggings cut into rectangles" %}

    Cut the fabric into large squares or rectangles. Mine were about 10" x 6".

  4. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/5-roll-all.jpg" alt="rectangles rolled into small rolls" %}

    Fold and roll up your rectangles so they will fit in the jar you've chosen.

  5. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/6-put-in-jar.jpg" alt="fabric rolls in a glass jar" %}

    Put all of the rolled up wipes into your jar.

  6. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/7-add-solution.jpg" alt="cloudy liquid being poured over fabric rolls in glass jar" %}

    Mix up your desired cleaning solution recipe from above and pour over the rolled up wipes.

  7. {% include image-source-set.html image="33-paper-towels/8-finished-wet-wipes.jpg" alt="jar full of wet rolled fabric" %}

    Give the material time to soak up the clothing solution. When ready, grab a wipe, squeeze out the excess liquid, and use as your would a regular wet wipe. These wet wipes can be washed and reused multiple times.
