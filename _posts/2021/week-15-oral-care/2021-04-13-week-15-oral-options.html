Bamboo toothbrush

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/bite-toothbrush.jpg" alt="bite brand bamboo toothbrush" %}

The average adult uses 300 toothbrushes in their lifetime. Instead of sending 300 plastic toothbrushes to a landfill, find a biodegradable bamboo one to brush those pearly whites. This bamboo toothbrush from Bite is 100% plant-based and biodegradable.

Toothpaste tablets

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/bite-toothpaste-bits.jpg" alt="bite brand toothpaste tablets" %}

Worldwide, 1.5 billion toothpaste tubes are discarded every year. Ditch the plastic tube and switch to toothpaste tablets. Bite Toothpaste Bits come in a reusable glass jar and refills come in a compostable bag. Not too keen on toothpaste tablets? Check out my list of 10 Sustainable Toothpaste Options for another alternative.

Plastic-free mouthwash

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/georganics-mouthwash.jpg" alt="aluminum bottle of georganics brand mouthwash" %}

Yes, mouthwash containers are typically recyclable, but since only 8% of plastics are actually recycled, it’s best to find a new swish-and-spit solution. Try this Oil Pulling Mouthwash by Georganics which comes in an infinitely recyclable aluminum bottle.

Silk floss

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/package-free-floss.jpg" alt="paper box of package free brand silk floss" %}

If you floss every day, you’ll use about five miles of floss over the course of your lifetime. Regular floss is made of plastic and some brands, like Oral-B Glide, use the same material found in Teflon. No thanks! Instead, opt for an all natural, 100% biodegradable silk floss like this spool sold at Package Free Shop.

Tongue scraper

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/package-free-tongue-scraper.jpg" alt="metallic tongue scrapper" %}

Tongue scraping removes bacteria, thus cleaning your mouth and cutting the bad breath. If you’re in the market for a tongue scraper, try this infinitely recyclable one from Clean Planetware. Don’t feel like shelling out cash for one? A spoon will also work!

Titanium toothpicks

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/package-free-toothpicks.jpg" alt="titanium toothpicks with Package Free brand case" %}

Wooden toothpicks aren’t just a waste of resources, they also break easily and can puncture your gums! Instead, grab a set of titanium toothpicks to keep your gums and our trees safe. These toothpicks from Package Free Shop come in a convenient keychain case and can be recycled with metals at end of life.

Reusable gum stimulator

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/gum-stimulator.jpg" alt="metal gum stimulator with pointy, rubber tip" %}

Gum stimulators are typically made of mixed plastic materials, making them impossible to recycle. If your dentist has encouraged you to go the gum stimulate route, grab a Gum Brand reusable metal one with replaceable rubber tips!

Teeth whitening gel

{% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/bite-whitening-gel.jpg" alt="Bite brand whitening gel with applicator box and brush" %}

Teeth whitening strips are pure plastic trash. Fortunately, Bite Whitening Gel is 100% plastic free!
