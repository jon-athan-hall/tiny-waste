Old floss containers are made of mixed materials that are difficult to recycle. Instead of tossing yours into the garbage, upcycle it into a cute travel sewing kit! The spool inside is perfect for adding thread and the sharp metal piece doubles as a convenient thread cutter, so you won't need scissors when you're out and about.

What you'll need

  • Old Floss Container
  • Old Kitchen Sponge
  • Scissors
  • Thread
  • Needle and other sewing notions
  • Optional sharpie to label kit

Let's make a sewing kit!

  1. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/1-get-floss.jpg" alt="Reach floss container" %}

    Get an old floss container. You can make a sewing kit from just about any shape.

  2. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/2-remove-label.jpg" alt="floss container with label removed" %}

    Remove the label. They come off easily, I didn't need any special tools.

  3. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/3-remove-piece.jpg" alt="floss container with inside piece taken out" %}

    Remove the inner plastic piece. It should pop right out.

  4. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/4-wrap-thread.jpg" alt="black thread wrapped around small plastic piece from inside floss container" %}

    Wrap your thread around the small round wheel where the floss usually goes. Choose a color that will be most usefill in your sewing kit and use as much as you like.

  5. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/5-thread-bobbin.jpg" alt="plastic wheel with thread put back on bigger plastic piece" %}

    Put your threaded wheel back on the bigger plastic piece and thread it as the floss would have been.

  6. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/6-insert-piece.jpg" alt="plastic piece put back into floss container" %}

    Put the now threaded plastic piece back into your floss container.

  7. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/7-cut-sponge.jpg" alt="small strip of kitchen sponge being cut" %}

    Cut a small strip of your sponge.

  8. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/8-add-sponge.jpg" alt="Sponge placed in lid of floss container" %}

    Fit the sponge inside the lid, trimming away excess so it fits in the lid of your floss container.

  9. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/9-add-notions.jpg" alt="needle and button added to sponge" %}

    Add a needle, button, and whatever other sewing notions you would like, to the sponge. You can cut small holes so that the piece will fit nice and snug.

  10. {% include image-source-set.html image="15-oral-care/10-finished-sewing-kit.jpg" alt="floss container transformed into sewing kit" %}

    If you'd like, use a sharpie to decorate the outside of your new sewing kit!
