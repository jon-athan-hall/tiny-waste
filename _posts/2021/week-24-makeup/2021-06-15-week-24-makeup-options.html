This week I took a good look at my beauty routine and its environmental impact. After taking a look at my makeup drawer, I realized just how much plastic waste I've produced over the last twenty years. As I use up the last of my conventional makeup, it's time to find a low-waste, eco-friendly replacement. Here are ten sustainable brands I've found that offer environmentally friendly packaging and natural ingredients.

What they offer: 3-in-1 Crayons that can be used on lips, cheeks, and eyes.

Price: One balmie starts at $14, 3 balmies for $36 ($6 savings), or 14 for $96 ($100 savings).

Elate Cosmetics

{% include image-source-set.html image="24-makeup/elate-cosmetics-makeup.jpg" alt="elate cosmetics brand eye shadow in bamboo case and bamboo brow brush" %}

What they offer: Everything

Price: Refill inserts start at $15

Elate Cosmetics carries everything you need for a sustainable makeup overhaul. They've done away with the majority of plastic and instead offer refillable bamboo compacts as well as recyclable aluminum and glass containers. They even have vegan makeup brushes with bamboo and aluminum handles.

As a great bonus, they offer sample kits so you can find your perfect concealer and foundation. The samples come in recyclable/reusable aluminum tins that are perfect for making your own lip balm.

What's Next

Now that you've decided what to put on your face, why not make the way you take it off a little better for the environment. Instead of toxic makeup remover, use something more natural like olive oil. And instead of disposable cotton rounds, make resuable ones with cotton fabric scraps.
