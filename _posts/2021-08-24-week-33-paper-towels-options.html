Final Thoughts:

I started using Dream Cloths, in the kitchen, as I find they work best with food messes since they can be rinsed and dry quickly. After some trial and error, I've learned they should be left where they can dry, otherwise they get a little slimy.

For other surfaces in the house, I use a cloth rag or resuable wet wipes. However, I do keep a box of bamboo paper towels on hand for grosser cleanups like vomit or pet mess.
